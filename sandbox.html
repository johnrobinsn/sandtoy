TeslaGL SandToy


A little pixel toy. Requires a browser supporting WebGL and the ability to do texture lookups from within a vertex shader. The particle physics are calculated on the GPU using a shader.

Just drag your finger around the picture to brush the pixels around... Like sand...

Here is a short youtube video of this running on my 2019 Model S along with some instruction to try this out on your own Tesla.
